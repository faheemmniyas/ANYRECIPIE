Incredients list:
  1. For rice:
    1. Take rice(300gm)
    2. Take water(5 cups)
    3. Rock salt
    4. oil/Ghee.
  2. For chole:
    1. Raw chole(250 gm)
    2. Chopped onions(2 onions)
    3. chilli
    4. Ginger garlic paste
    5. Spices: Chole Masala, Garam Masala
    6. Salt, Black Salt
Receipe:
  1. For dough:
    1. Take rice add water and pinch of Rock salt, 1 spoon of ghee and mix it well
    2. Keep the wok at boil for 20 minutes.
    3. Now rinse it out
    4. Keep dry cloth above to absorb extra moisture..
  2. For Chole Gravy:
    1. Soak chole and boil in water
    2. In cooker add 2 table spoon refined oil, chopped onions along with spices and chilli and cook it for 5 minutes in medium flame then 2 minutes in low flame.
    3. Stir the mixture well and add salt,ginger garlic paste and chole masala
    4. Cook it for next 5 minutes.
Set the rice and chole curry in an plate in mesmerizing way.
