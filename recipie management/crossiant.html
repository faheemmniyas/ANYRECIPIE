Incredients list:
  1. For Crossiant:
    1. Take flour(800gm)
    2. Take water(5 cups)
    3. salt
    4. Yeast.
  2. For Filling:
    1. Butter(250 gm)
    2. Sugar(2 onions)
    3. Vanilla essence
    4. Vanilla powder
    5. rose powder
    6. Pinch of butterscothc essence
Receipe:
  1. For Crossiant:
    1. Take flour add water and pinch of salt, 1 spoon of yeast in warm water and mix it to form well structured dough
    2. Keep the dough at rest for 3 hrs.
    3. Now cut it in small parts and roll each part into cylinder sheets
    4. Heat oven in medium temperature.
  2. For filling:
    1. Add butter and sugar and whisk it well
    2. mix it with the vannilla essence and powder.
    3. then stir it with rose powder
    4. Finally add the butterscoth essence.
Roll the crossiant with the Filling and bake it at 220 degree for 15 minutes.
