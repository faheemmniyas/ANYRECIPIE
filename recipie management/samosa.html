Incredients list:
  1. For dough:
    1. Take flour(800gm)
    2. Take water(5 cups)
    3. Rock salt
    4. oil/Ghee.
  2. For filling:
    1. Raw potato(250 gm)
    2. Chopped onions(2 onions)
    3. chilli
    4. Ginger garlic paste
    5. Spices: Chole Masala, Garam Masala
    6. Salt, Black Salt
Receipe:
  1. For dough:
    1. Take flour add water and pinch of Rock salt, 1 spoon of ghee and mix it to form well structured dough
    2. Keep the dough at rest for 2 hrs.
    3. Now cut it in small parts and roll each part into circular sheets
    4. Heat oil in frying pan and fry the bhatura in medium flame.
  2. For Chole Gravy:
    1. Soak potato and boil in water
    2. In cooker add 2 table spoon refined oil, chopped onions along with spices and chilli and cook it for 5 minutes in medium flame then 2 minutes in low flame.
    3. Stir the mixture well and add salt,ginger garlic paste and samosa masala
    4. Cook it for next 5 minutes.
Fry the dough with the fliing inside.
